Candidate Profile: Maelor the Spellwright

“I don’t raise my voice. The orb speaks louder.”

About

Maelor is a senior spellwright from the Obsidian Archives. Known for his stoicism and precision, he’s led enchantment regulation reform across the Flame Union.

Key Campaign Policies

  • Orb-handling licenses for all apprentices
  • Universal hazard pay for night-casting
  • Ban on unstable enchantments in public places
