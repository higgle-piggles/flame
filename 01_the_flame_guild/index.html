🔥Campaign: The Right to Rest (And Respawn)

We are tired of being tired. Mages, smiths, egineers and potion brewers are working long hours without proper breaks. We are not asking for magic - just a momment to eat our stew in peace

Our 3 Main Campaign Goals:

  1. The 4 hour rule - Every worker gets a break every 4 hours. Even during dragon sieges
  2. The Universal - Bedroll Acess - Free flameproof bedrolls for all guild-certified workers
  3. Respawn Leave - If you're ressurected due to unsafe working conditions, you get the day off. No agurments.
